About PEDIASCAPE

Empowering students with tailored guidance, resources, and AI-powered assistance for academic excellence.

Our Mission

EduPath was created by five passionate students from Bharat Institute of Engineering and Technology with one goal in mind: to provide undergraduate students with the guidance and resources they need to excel in their academic journey.

Vision

To create a comprehensive platform where every undergraduate student can access quality learning resources, personalized roadmaps, and expert guidance to achieve their academic goals.

Our Approach

We believe in structured learning paths, quality resources, and personalized guidance. Our platform combines these elements with cutting-edge AI technology to provide a holistic learning experience.

What Sets Us Apart

Created by students for students, our platform understands the challenges undergraduate students face. We offer tailored solutions including AI-assisted doubt clearing, curated study materials, and strategic roadmaps.

Our Story

Born from our own experiences as undergraduate students at Bharat Institute of Engineering and Technology, we identified critical gaps in the support system for exam preparation and career planning. What started as a small project to help our peers has evolved into a comprehensive platform designed to support students throughout their academic journey.


We combined our diverse skills in technology, content development, and educational psychology to create an integrated solution that addresses the multifaceted needs of undergraduate students. Our platform has been built with love, dedication, and a deep understanding of the challenges students face.
